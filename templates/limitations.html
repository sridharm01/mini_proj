Limitations


Accuracy of Results

While our AI technology is advanced, it may not always provide 100% accurate diagnoses. It is important to follow up with a healthcare professional for confirmation.

Image Quality

The accuracy of our analysis depends on the quality of the images you provide. Ensure your photos are well-lit, focused, and clear for the best results.

Medical advice not replaced

SkinSpectra is designed to assist in identifying potential skin issues, but it is not a replacement for a professional medical evaluation. Always consult with a healthcare provider for a comprehensive assessment.

Data Privacy

We prioritize your privacy and take measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely immune to security breaches. Use our service with an understanding of these risks.

Limited to Certain Conditions

Our platform currently identifies seven types of skin lesions and normal skin. It may not detect other types of skin conditions or diseases.


Disclaimer

SkinSpectra is a tool intended to assist with the early detection of potential skin issues. It should be used in conjunction with, and not as a substitute for, professional medical advice. Always seek the guidance of qualified health professionals with any questions you may have regarding your health or a medical condition.
